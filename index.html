01 — Overview

The short version

🔒

UNSILENCE does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. Everything runs entirely on your device. Nothing leaves it.

UNSILENCE is a silent mode reminder app. You set a timer, we remind you to unmute when it ends. That's the entire app. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no internet connection of any kind.

03 — Notifications

How notifications work

UNSILENCE uses iOS local notifications to remind you to unmute your phone. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely by iOS on your device. No notification content is sent to any external server.

You can revoke notification permission at any time in Settings → UNSILENCE → Notifications. The app will still function — you just won't receive the reminder.

04 — Third Parties

Third-party services

UNSILENCE uses no third-party services, advertising networks, analytics tools, or SDKs of any kind. There are no integrations that could collect data on our behalf.

05 — Children

Children's privacy

Since UNSILENCE collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

06 — Changes

Policy updates

If this policy ever changes — for example if a future version of UNSILENCE adds features that involve data — we will update this page and the "Updated" date in the header above. We will never collect data without updating this policy first.
